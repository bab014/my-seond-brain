#  Creamy Banana Smoothie

A creamy, filling and sweet smoothie

```yaml
ingredients:
    - 2 frozen bananas
    - 1/2 cup of yoghurt
    - 1 cup of coconut milk
    - 1 serving of protein powder
instructions:
    - Place all ingredients in a high speed blender and blend
```
